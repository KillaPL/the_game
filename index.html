README
So that's what is going on here:
A bunch of people decided to build a small village:
Currently, a leader, woodcutter, fisherman and 2 gatherers.
They eat berries from ground, they catch fishes and cook them, they drink water from the river
And they have to sleep.
They also are building their first construction, a dormitory in which they then sleep.
The tiles mean:
"~" -> river
"." -> regular, empty field
"t" -> tree
"#" -> berries bush
blue rectangle -> dormitory being built
brown rectangle -> done dormitory
"L" -> leader
"W" -> woodcutter
"F" -> fisherman
"G" -> gatherer

Changelog, 16.10.2014
  • Complete graphics overhaul.
    Sprites were provided by Sebastian Jackowski, who is now officially chief of arts.
  • New wood management.
    Once tree is cut, it is transformed into a pile of woods. These are then brought to stash by woodcutter. This is a first step into introducing new building: carpenter workshop, which will produce goods from logs
  • Waterskins.
    People now wear waterskins. They are able to hold 1 liter of water in them. When they feel thirsty, they first drink from waterskins, and just when it's empty, they go to river to refill it.
  • Personal inventory.
    Each person carries some inventory of his own. It is now printed and can be checked.
